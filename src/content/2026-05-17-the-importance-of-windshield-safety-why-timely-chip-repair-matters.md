# Introduction to Windshield Safety 
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A small chip or crack can quickly turn into a larger problem, compromising the structural integrity of your windshield and putting you and your passengers at risk. 
## Why Chip Repair is Crucial 
* Prevents further damage: Chips and cracks can spread rapidly, especially when exposed to temperature changes, road vibrations, or other environmental factors. 
* Ensures visibility: A damaged windshield can obstruct your view of the road, increasing the likelihood of accidents. 
* Saves money: Repairing chips and cracks is significantly cheaper than replacing the entire windshield.
