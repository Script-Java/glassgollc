# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you and your passengers at risk. In this article, we will explore the importance of windshield safety, causes of damage, prevention methods, and repair options.
## Causes of Windshield Damage
* Debris on the road, such as rocks and stones
* Extreme weather conditions, like hail or heavy snow
* Collision with another vehicle or object
* Poor windshield maintenance
## Prevention Methods
To minimize the risk of windshield damage, follow these tips:
* Regularly inspect your windshield for chips and cracks
* Avoid driving in hazardous weather conditions
* Keep a safe distance from other vehicles
* Use high-quality windshield wipers and replace them regularly
