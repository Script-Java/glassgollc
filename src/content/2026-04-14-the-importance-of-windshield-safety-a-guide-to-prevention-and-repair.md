      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance, and it's essential to understand the risks associated with damaged windshields. A cracked or broken windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you and your passengers at risk of injury.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      * A damaged windshield can obstruct your view, increasing the likelihood of accidents
      * Windshields play a crucial role in maintaining the vehicle's airbag system
      * A cracked windshield can shatter during an accident, causing severe injuries

      ## Prevention is Key
      To prevent windshield damage, it's essential to:
      + Avoid driving over potholes and speed bumps
      + Park in shaded areas to reduce the risk of thermal stress
      + Use a windshield sunshade to block direct sunlight
