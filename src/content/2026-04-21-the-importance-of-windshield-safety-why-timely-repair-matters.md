# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance, often overlooked until a crack or chip appears. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, posing a significant risk to occupants in the event of an accident.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
*   **Structural Support**: The windshield provides up to 30% of the vehicle's structural support, crucial in rollover accidents.
*   **Airbag Deployment**: A intact windshield ensures proper airbag deployment, directing the airbag towards the occupant.
## Benefits of Timely Repair
*   **Prevents Further Damage**: Repairing chips and cracks early prevents them from spreading, reducing the need for full windshield replacement.
*   **Cost Savings**: Timely repair is significantly less expensive than replacing the entire windshield.
